about

At the heart of our operations in Kapolei, Hawaii - ROCAMOCHA is working hard to bring sonic refreshment to your brainspace through music.

Using the knowledge acquired through his studies as a presidential scholar at the Berklee College of Music, ROCAMOCHA carefully extracts the ingredients from the tastiest and also the cutest music to deliver a combination of sounds that are sure to make you smile!

ROCAMOCHA's first single, The Perfect Smoothie was the first ever cyoot experiment, conducted with help from Malaysian violinist and vocalist Nisa Addina (a.k.a Lyrit). The very first Cyoot Labs EP, Bubble Magic, featuring 5 cyoot experiments by ROCAMOCHA is scheduled to release September 23rd.
